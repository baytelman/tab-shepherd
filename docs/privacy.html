Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 8, 2025

Tab Shepherd ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our browser extension.

Summary: Tab Shepherd does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your configuration data stays locally in your browser.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Locally

Tab Shepherd stores the following data locally in your browser using Chrome's Storage API:

  • Window Groups Configuration: Names and URL patterns you configure for organizing tabs
  • Extension Settings: Your preferences for how the extension operates

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us or any third party.

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your browsing history or URLs you visit
  • Personal identification information
  • Account credentials or passwords
  • Tab contents or page data
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Any data from other extensions

2. Browser Permissions

Tab Shepherd requests the following browser permissions:

Permission Purpose Data Access
tabs Read tab URLs to match against your configured patterns and move tabs between windows Tab URLs are processed locally only; never transmitted
tabGroups Manage tab groups within windows Group information stays local
storage Save your configuration and preferences Stored locally in your browser

3. Analytics and Usage Data

Future Analytics: We may implement anonymous usage analytics in future versions to help improve the Extension. If implemented, this would include:

  • Aggregate, anonymized usage statistics (e.g., feature usage counts)
  • Extension version and browser type
  • Error reports to help fix bugs

Any future analytics implementation will:

  • Never include personally identifiable information
  • Never include your browsing history, URLs, or tab contents
  • Never include your configuration patterns
  • Be disclosed in an updated Privacy Policy
  • Potentially offer an opt-out mechanism

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share any data with third parties. Since we don't collect personal data, there is nothing to share.

5. Data Security

Your configuration data is stored using Chrome's built-in Storage API, which provides:

  • Encryption at rest on your device
  • Isolation from other extensions and websites
  • Optional sync across your Chrome browsers (if you have Chrome Sync enabled)

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is stored locally until you:

  • Uninstall the Extension (automatically deletes all Extension data)
  • Clear your browser data
  • Manually delete configurations through the Extension's options page

7. Children's Privacy

Tab Shepherd is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Third-Party Services

Tab Shepherd does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or external servers in its current version.

9. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data through the Extension's options page
  • Export: Export your configuration using the built-in export feature
  • Delete: Remove all data by uninstalling the Extension or clearing browser data
  • Portability: Import/export your configuration as JSON files

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • Including release notes with Extension updates

Your continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Open Source

Tab Shepherd's source code is available for review, allowing you to verify our privacy practices.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through the Extension's support page on the Chrome Web Store.
